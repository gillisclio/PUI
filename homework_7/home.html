What's More...

Honeybees polinate 70 of the around 100 crop species that feed 90% of the world.

They’re also responsible for $30 billion a year in crops.

(Not to mention that almonds literally would not exist without honeybees to pollinate almond trees.)

Unfortunately, the future of the honeybee is at risk.
